Forget-Me-Nots

Forget-me-nots are considered "attractive flowers". Their blooms are usually blue (like the photo) but pink, white, and yellow do exist!

Normal Sunlight Consistent Moist Soil Warm/Cold Temp
Sunflowers

Sunflowers are big, beauitful flowers. They are perfect in Summer since they are heat-tolerant, have a resistant to pests, and attract pollinators and birds

Full Sunlight Well-Drained Soil Warm/Hot Temp
Tulips

Tulips are jewels that brighten our days in Spring, you can actually grow them in Winter due to them being pretty cold-tolerant. They also come in many different colors like yellow, orange, purple and many many more!

Full Sunlight Well-Drained Soil Warm Temp
